Frequently Asked Questions

Is AnimSpoof safe to use?
Yes! AnimSpoof is completely safe as it only changes animations on your client side. Other players will still see your original animations, so there's no risk of breaking Roblox's Terms of Service.
Will I get banned for using AnimSpoof?
No, AnimSpoof is designed to be undetectable and only affects what you see locally. You won't get banned for using our software.
Does it work with all Roblox games?
AnimSpoof works with most Roblox games that use the default animation system. Some games with heavy custom animations may have limited compatibility.
How do I update to the latest version?
AnimSpoof automatically checks for updates when you launch it. If a new version is available, you'll be prompted to download and install it with one click.
